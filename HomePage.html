Nintendo Co., Ltd. is a Japanese multinational video game company headquartered in Kyoto. It develops, publishes, and releases both video games and video game consoles. Nintendo developed its first arcade games in the 1970s. Since then, Nintendo has produced some of the most successful consoles in the video game industry, including the Game Boy (1989), the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (1991), the Nintendo DS (2004), the Wii (2006), and the Nintendo Switch (2017). It has created or published numerous major franchises, including Mario, Metroid, The Legend of Zelda, Pikmin and Pok√©mon.
Stats: Nintendo's Net Sales and Net Income
In 2024, Nintendo's net sales amounted to just over $11 billion U.S. dollars, down from over $12 billion a year earlier, while Nintendo has a reported net income of $3.25 billion U.S. dollars, the same as the previous year and down from $3.9 billion U.S. dollars reported in 2022.
