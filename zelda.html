The series centers on the various incarnations of Link, a courageous young man of the elf-like Hylian race, and Princess Zelda, a princess within the bloodline of the goddess Hylia, as they fight to save the land of Hyrule from Ganon, an evil warlord turned demon king, who is the principal antagonist of the series. Ganon plans to use the Triforce, a sacred relic, to remake the world in his dark image. The Triforce's power can grant any wish its user desires when gathered, highlighting the series' protagonists' journey to save Hyrule.
Stats: Total and the Latest Released Game Sales
Released in May 2023, the latest release in The Legend of Zelda series, Tears of the Kingdom, sold over 10 million copies during its first three days of release and currently stands at a total of 20.61 million units sold. In total, the entire Legend of Zelda series has sold over 150 million copies, making it one of the most popular gaming series worldwide.
