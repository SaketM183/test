Pikmin is a real-time strategy and puzzle video game series by Shigeru Miyamoto, published by Nintendo. The series involves directing a horde of plant-like creatures called Pikmin to collect items by destroying obstacles, avoiding hazards, and fighting hazardous fauna. The series has four main entries, including Pikmin (2001) and Pikmin 2 (2004), released for GameCube, Wii, and Nintendo Switch. The third game, Pikmin 3, was released for Wii U in 2013, and Pikmin 4 for Nintendo Switch in 2023.
Stats: Pikmin Games Sales
The original Pikmin sold 1.6 million copies, followed by Pikmin 2 with 1.12 million and Pikmin 3 with 1.28 million. The series reached a new peak with Pikmin 4, which sold an impressive 3.48 million copies. As of March 2024, the franchise has sold over 11.37 million copies worldwide, marking it as a significant success for Nintendo.
